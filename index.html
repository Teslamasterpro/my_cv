01 — About

Hello there.

I'm Aditya Patel, a mechatronics engineer studying and living in Germany. I work where software meets hardware: teaching robots to see, writing firmware for lab instruments, and designing the mechanical parts in between.

Away from the workbench I travel and hike, chase live music, play guitar, and take video games more seriously than I probably should.

Currently: research master's at THWS Schweinfurt, open to collaborations.
